# How to Upload Documents

Only Admins and Sub-Admins can upload documents.

1. Log in to your account.
2. Click 'Upload Document' on the home page or dashboard.
3. Select the jurisdiction and PDF file.
4. Click 'Upload'.
5. Wait for processing (status: processing, ready, or failed).
6. Once ready, the document is available for citizen queries.

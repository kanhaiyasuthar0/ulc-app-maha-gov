# Roles & Permissions

## Admin (IAS Officer)
- Full control over the portal
- Manage sub-admins
- Oversee all documents
- Ensure compliance and security

## Sub-Admin
- Manage assigned jurisdictions
- Upload documents for their region
- Keep the portal updated

## Citizen (Consumer)
- Any citizen can ask questions about public documents
- No login required
- Get instant answers and access government information

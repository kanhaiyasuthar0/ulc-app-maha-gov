# Frequently Asked Questions

## Who can upload documents?
Only Admins and Sub-Admins can upload documents.

## Do I need to log in to ask questions?
No, citizens can ask questions without logging in.

## What languages are supported?
Marathi, English, and more.

## How are answers generated?
AI retrieves and summarizes information from uploaded government documents.

## How do I preview the source PDF?
Click 'View Source PDF' in the chat answer to see the original document.
